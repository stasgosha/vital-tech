Long-acting Energy

Add-on formulation for long-acting healthy energy.

In contrast to conventional energy drinks, the add-on formulation implements controlled and sustained release capabilities to deliver significant dosing of energy-promoting bioactives, “stretched” along 4 – 6 hours

Moreover, no carbohydrates or other calorie-rich nutrients’ supplementation are required to enjoy the add-on formulation’s long-acting, healthy energy properties

The formulation series is optimal for incorporation into health, energy and protein bars, protein shakes, powdered meal replacement products; and; powdered mix-n-drink beverages

Sports Nutrition

Primarily targeting amateur athletes, and providing them an array of powerful functional formulations for intra-exercise performance enhancement; and; rapid post-exercise recovery.

The formulation series can be applied to the following athlete usage profiles:

  • Loading (single period)
  • Pre-exercise body preparation
  • Short exercise duration (under 60m) performance enhancement
  • Long duration (60m+) intra-exercise performance enhancement
  • Post-exercise effective recovery

The formulation series is optimal for incorporation into health, energy and protein bars, protein shakes, powdered meal replacement products; and; powdered mix-n-drink beverages
